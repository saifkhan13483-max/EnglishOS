## Module 3: Basic Sentences — Ek Formula, Sab Sentences
*(Din 13 – 20)*

### 20/80 Rule Applied:
> **Sirf 1 formula seekhne se tum koi bhi English sentence bana sakte ho.**

### THE MASTER FORMULA:

```
┌─────────────┬──────────────┬──────────────┐
│   SUBJECT   │     VERB     │    OBJECT    │
│ (Kaam karne │   (Kaam)     │  (Jis par    │
│    wala)    │              │   kaam ho)   │
└─────────────┴──────────────┴──────────────┘
```

**Misaalein:**

| Subject | Verb | Object | Roman Urdu |
|---------|------|--------|-----------|
| I | eat | food. | Mein khaana khata hoon. |
| She | reads | books. | Woh kitaabein parhti hai. |
| He | drinks | water. | Woh paani peeta hai. |
| We | play | cricket. | Hum cricket khelte hain. |
| They | watch | TV. | Woh TV dekhte hain. |
| The dog | runs | fast. | Kutta tez bhaagta hai. |

---

### Sentence Type 1: POSITIVE (Haan wali baat)

**Formula: Subject + Verb + Object**

```
I am happy.           → Mein khush hoon.
She is a doctor.      → Woh doctor hai.
He works every day.   → Woh roz kaam karta hai.
We eat dinner at 8.   → Hum raat 8 baje khana khate hain.
They live in Karachi. → Woh Karachi mein rehte hain.
```

---

### Sentence Type 2: NEGATIVE (Nahi wali baat)

**Formula: Subject + do not / does not / is not + Verb**

```
NOT rules:
  I/You/We/They  → do not (don't)
  He/She/It      → does not (doesn't)
  I              → am not
  He/She         → is not (isn't)
  We/They        → are not (aren't)
```

**Misaalein:**
```
I do not eat meat.         → Mein gosht nahi khata.
She does not go out.       → Woh bahar nahi jaati.
He is not here today.      → Woh aaj yahan nahi hai.
We are not ready yet.      → Hum abhi tayyar nahi hain.
They don't study at night. → Woh raat ko nahi parhte.
```

---

### Sentence Type 3: QUESTION (Sawaal)

**Formula: Do/Does/Is/Are + Subject + Verb + ?**

```
Do you like tea?          → Kya tum chai pasand karte ho?
Does she eat rice?        → Kya woh chawal khati hai?
Is he your brother?       → Kya woh tumhara bhai hai?
Are they students?        → Kya woh students hain?
Where do you live?        → Tum kahan rehte ho?
What does she want?       → Woh kya chahti hai?
When do you wake up?      → Tum kab uthte ho?
How are you?              → Tum kaise ho?
```

## Module 6: Present Tense — Abhi ki Baat
*(Din 31 – 40)*

### 20/80 Rule: Present Tense ke 2 Types hi kafi hain

---

### Present Simple — Roz hone wali baatein

**Kab use karein:** Jo kaam roz hota hai, hamesha hota hai, ya sach hai

**Formula:**
```
I / You / We / They + Verb (base form)
He / She / It       + Verb + s/es
```

**"s" kab lagao?**
```
He/She/It ke saath: work → works, eat → eats, go → goes
Exceptions: have → has, do → does
```

**Misaalein:**
```
I eat breakfast every morning.    → Mein roz subah naashta karta hoon.
You go to school by bus.          → Tum bus se school jaate ho.
He reads newspaper daily.         → Woh roz akhbaar parhta hai.
She teaches English at school.    → Woh school mein English parhati hai.
We play cricket on Sundays.       → Hum Sunday ko cricket khelte hain.
They live in a big house.         → Woh ek bare ghar mein rehte hain.
```

**Negative:**
```
I don't like cold weather.        → Mujhe thanda mausam pasand nahi.
He doesn't eat spicy food.        → Woh tez masale nahi khata.
She doesn't watch TV at night.    → Woh raat ko TV nahi dekhti.
```

**Questions:**
```
Do you speak English?             → Kya aap English bolte hain?
Does he live near here?           → Kya woh yahan qareeb rehta hai?
What time do you wake up?         → Tum kab uthte ho?
Where does she work?              → Woh kahan kaam karti hai?
```

**Time Expressions (Waqt batane ke alfaaz):**
```
every day        → roz
always           → hamesha
usually          → aksar
often            → zyadatar
sometimes        → kabhi kabhi
never            → kabhi nahi
on Mondays       → Peer ko
in the morning   → subah ko
```

---

### Present Continuous — Abhi ho raha hai

**Kab use karein:** Jo kaam is waqt ho raha hai

**Formula:**
```
Subject + am/is/are + Verb + ing
```

**am/is/are ka Rule:**
```
I        → am + verb+ing      (I am eating.)
He/She   → is + verb+ing      (She is sleeping.)
You/We   → are + verb+ing     (We are playing.)
```

**Verb mein "ing" kaise lagayein:**
```
Normal words:    eat → eating,  read → reading
"e" pe khatam:  make → making,  come → coming (e hata do)
Short words:     run → running,  sit → sitting (last letter double)
```

**Misaalein:**
```
I am writing a letter right now.     → Mein abhi ek khat likh raha hoon.
She is cooking dinner in the kitchen.→ Woh kitchen mein dinner paka rahi hai.
He is watching a match.              → Woh match dekh raha hai.
We are studying for the exam.        → Hum imtehaan ki tayyari kar rahe hain.
They are playing in the park.        → Woh park mein khel rahe hain.
```

**Negative:**
```
I am not sleeping, I am studying.    → Mein so nahi raha, parh raha hoon.
She is not crying, she is laughing.  → Woh ro nahi rahi, hans rahi hai.
```

**Question:**
```
Are you listening to me?             → Kya aap meri baat sun rahe hain?
Is she coming to the party?          → Kya woh party mein aa rahi hai?
What are you doing right now?        → Abhi tum kya kar rahe ho?
```

## Module 9: Articles — A, An, The
*(Din 56 – 60)*

### Sirf 2 Rules Yaad Karo:

**Rule 1 — A ya An: Pehli baar kisi cheez ka zikr**
```
A  → Consonant sound se shuru hone wale words
An → Vowel sound se shuru hone wale words (A, E, I, O, U sounds)
```

**A ki misaalein:**
```
a book    (B consonant)     a car     (C consonant)
a dog     (D consonant)     a friend  (F consonant)
a university (U = "yoo" sound, consonant!)
```

**An ki misaalein:**
```
an apple    (A vowel)       an egg      (E vowel)
an idea     (I vowel)       an orange   (O vowel)
an umbrella (U vowel)       an hour     (H silent, O sound)
an honest man (H silent, O sound)
```

**Rule 2 — The: Kisi khaas cheez ka zikr**
```
Jab dono ko pata ho kaunsi cheez:
  Please open the door.         → Darwaza kholo. (Kaunsa? Saamne wala)
  The sun rises in the east.    → Suraj mashriq mein ugta hai.
  I saw a dog. The dog was big. → 2nd baar wahi dog

Unique cheezein (sirf ek hoti hain):
  the sun, the moon, the earth, the sky
```

**The nahi lagate:**
```
Proper names:  Ali, Pakistan, Lahore (not "the Ali")
Languages:     English, Urdu (not "the English")
Meals:         breakfast, lunch, dinner (not "the lunch")
Sports:        cricket, football (not "the cricket")
```

| He is more better. | He is better. | "more" + comparative double hai |
| I am agree. | I agree. | agree verb hai, "am" nahi lagta |
| Since 3 years. | For 3 years. | duration = for |
| Discuss about it. | Discuss it. | discuss already "about" include karta hai |
| Return back. | Return. | "back" already included |
| Today morning. | This morning. | Today morning galat hai |
| I am fine. (hamesha) | I am doing well. | More natural |
| Very very good. | Excellent / Outstanding. | Repetition avoid karo |

## Module 18: Conditionals — Agar... To...
*(Din 181 – 195)*

### 3 Types:

**Type 1 — Real / Possible (Ho sakta hai):**
```
Formula: If + Present Simple, will + Verb

If it rains, I will stay home.           → Agar baarish hui to ghar rahonga.
If you study hard, you will pass.        → Agar mehnat ki to pass ho jaoge.
If I have time, I will help you.         → Agar waqt mila to madad karoonga.
If she calls, tell her I am busy.        → Agar woh call kare to batao mein busy hoon.
```

**Type 2 — Imaginary (Kash hota):**
```
Formula: If + Past Simple, would + Verb

If I were rich, I would travel the world.→ Kash mein ameer hota to duniya dekhta.
If she knew the answer, she would tell.  → Agar use pata hota to woh batati.
If I had a car, I would drive to Lahore. → Agar gaadi hoti to Lahore jaata.
```

**Type 3 — Impossible Past (Jo hua hi nahi):**
```
Formula: If + had + V3, would have + V3

If I had studied, I would have passed.   → Agar parha hota to pass ho jaata.
If she had slept early, she would not have been tired. → Agar jaldi soyi hoti...
```

## Module 19: Passive Voice
*(Din 196 – 205)*

**Active:** Kaam karne wala saamne hai
**Passive:** Kaam karne wala chhupa hua hai — kaam khud important hai

```
Active:  Ali wrote this letter.           → Ali ne yeh khat likha.
Passive: This letter was written by Ali.  → Yeh khat Ali ne likha (passive).

Active:  They will complete the project.
Passive: The project will be completed.

Active:  I eat food.
Passive: Food is eaten (by me).
```

**Formula:**
```
Object + is/am/are/was/were/will be + V3 (+ by + Subject)
```

Ali Hassan

### Interview English:

My name is ___. I have completed my ___ from ___.

### Writing Task 1 — Graph/Chart Describe Karna:

**Opening:**
> The graph/chart shows ___. It illustrates the changes in ___ over the period ___.

**Key Phrases:**
```
There was a significant increase in...   → Bohot zyada izaafa hua
There was a sharp decline in...          → Bohot zyada kami aayi
remained stable / unchanged              → Theek waise hi raha
peaked at / reached its highest point    → Sab se zyada tha

```

### Writing Task 2 — Opinion Essay:

**Structure:**
```
Introduction   → Topic + Your opinion (2–3 sentences)
Body 1         → First reason + example (5–6 sentences)
Body 2         → Second reason + example (5–6 sentences)
Conclusion     → Restate your opinion + final thought (2–3 sentences)
```

**Sample Essay Opening:**
> In today's world, many people believe that technology has had a negative impact on human relationships. While I partially agree with this view, I believe that technology, when used wisely, can actually strengthen human connections.

## Module 25: Fluency Building — Daily Habits
*(Din 291 – 300)*

### 7 Daily Habits for Fluency:

**1. Think in English (Sochna English mein):**
> Roz 10 minute ke liye apne khayal English mein socho. Urdu mein translate mat karo — seedha English.

**2. Talk to Yourself (Khud se baat karo):**
> Jab akele ho, apni activities describe karo:
> "I am making tea now. I will add two spoons of sugar. The tea is ready."

**3. Shadow Speak (Copy karo):**
> English videos dekho (BBC, YouTube) aur ek ek sentence unke saath dohrao — same tone, same speed.

**4. Word Journal (Alfaaz ki diary):**
> Roz 3 naye words likhna, unka matlab, aur ek sentence.

**5. English News (Khabar Angrezi mein):**
> BBC Urdu English section ya Dawn News — roz 10 minute padho.

**6. Describe Pictures (Tasveer batao):**
> Koi bhi photo dekho aur 5 sentences mein describe karo English mein.

**7. Record Yourself (Apni awaaz sunno):**
> Khud ko record karo 1 minute mein koi bhi topic par bolta hua. Phir suno — mistakes khud samjh aayengi.
